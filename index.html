For your eyes only

A PIN and biometrics guard the vault. A second, decoy PIN opens a separate one — so what you unlock under pressure is not what you actually keep.

☁️

Backups you control

Encrypted backups go to your iCloud or Google Drive, sealed with your own passphrase before they leave the device. Never automatic, never through us.

🛡️

Encrypted on the device

AES-256 at rest, with the key held in the iOS Keychain or Android KeyStore and marked device-only. We operate no backend — see the privacy policy.

One thing to know up front: your backup passphrase cannot be recovered — not by us, not by anyone. That is what makes the backup safe to keep in your own cloud, and it is also why you should write the passphrase down somewhere you trust.
